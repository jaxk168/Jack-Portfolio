About Me

I'm Jack, a passionate AMV editor with over 5 years of experience transforming anime into visual music experiences.

My work focuses on synchronizing powerful moments with impactful music, creating emotional journeys that resonate with viewers.

My Journey

How It Started

My passion for AMVs began when I discovered anime in primary school. Fascinated by how music could transform animated scenes, I started experimenting with basic video editing software. What began as a hobby quickly evolved into a creative outlet where I could express my interpretation of anime narratives through carefully selected music.

Evolution

As my skills improved, I transitioned to professional editing software and began developing my unique style. I focused on mastering the technical aspects of video editing while refining my approach to storytelling. Each project became an opportunity to push my creative boundaries and explore new techniques.

Recognition

After years of dedication, my work began gaining recognition in online communities. The feedback and support from viewers continue to inspire me to create more ambitious projects.

Today

Currently, I'm focused on pushing the boundaries of visual effects in my AMVs. I'm constantly learning new editing techniques and exploring different music genres to create fresh, emotionally impactful videos. I also enjoy collaborating with other creators and sharing knowledge with the AMV community.

My Editing Philosophy

Emotion First

I believe the most powerful AMVs evoke genuine emotion. Whether it's excitement, nostalgia, or melancholy, creating an emotional connection between the viewer, the music, and the visuals is my primary goal for every project.

Technical Excellence

While emotion drives my work, technical precision brings it to life. I'm meticulous about timing, transitions, color grading, and effects. Every frame should serve the story and enhance the viewing experience.

Respect for Source Material

I approach each anime with respect for its original themes and storytelling. My goal is to highlight and sometimes reinterpret these elements through music, not to undermine the creator's vision.

Continuous Growth

Every project is an opportunity to learn and improve. I embrace feedback and continuously study new techniques to elevate my work. The journey of mastering this art form never ends.
